Your Legacy of Compassion

Planned giving allows you to make a significant future gift to Our Kind Kitchen while maintaining your current financial security. Your legacy will continue to serve neighbors in need for years to come.

Imagine Your Impact

A legacy gift can provide hundreds or even thousands of meals to families facing difficult times, creating a ripple effect of kindness that extends far beyond your lifetime.

Ways to Leave Your Legacy

Bequests

Include Our Kind Kitchen in your will or living trust. You can designate a specific amount, percentage of your estate, or the remainder after other bequests.

"I give to Our Kind Kitchen, EIN 99-3415090, [amount or percentage] for its general purposes."

Life Insurance

Name Our Kind Kitchen as a beneficiary of your life insurance policy - either in whole or in part. This is a simple way to make a significant future gift.

Retirement Plans

Designate Our Kind Kitchen as a beneficiary of your IRA, 401(k), or other retirement accounts. This can be a tax-smart way to leave a legacy.

Benefits of Planned Giving

Maintain Current Assets

Continue to use and enjoy your assets during your lifetime while making arrangements for a future gift.

Tax Benefits

Planned gifts may provide estate tax benefits and reduce the burden on your heirs. Consult your financial advisor.

Flexibility

Many planned gifts can be modified if your circumstances change, providing peace of mind.
