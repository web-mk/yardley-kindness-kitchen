Cook With Us

Join our vibrant community of volunteers in the kitchen! Our cooking sessions are a wonderful opportunity to meet new people, learn new recipes, and make a tangible difference in someone's life.

What to Expect:

  • 2-3 hour cooking sessions
  • All ingredients and equipment provided
  • Work alongside experienced volunteers
  • Prepare 10-15 gourmet meals per session
  • Learn new cooking techniques and recipes

Deliver For Us

Our delivery volunteers are the bridge between our kitchen and the families we serve. This is a flexible opportunity that typically takes 30-60 minutes.

Requirements:

  • Valid driver's license and personal vehicle
  • Auto insurance
  • Ability to lift and carry meals
  • GPS/smartphone for navigation
  • Willingness to cover your own fuel costs

What You'll Do:

  • Pick up prepared meals from our kitchen
  • Deliver to 1-3 families in the area
  • Optional: brief friendly visit with recipients
