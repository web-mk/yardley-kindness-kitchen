Who We Serve

We provide gourmet, home-cooked meals to individuals and families facing:

  • Medical Emergencies or recovery from surgery/treatment
  • New Parenthood - the "fourth trimester" and beyond
  • Senior Support for elderly neighbors who may be lonely or struggling with mobility
  • Difficult Life Transitions where a little extra care is needed
